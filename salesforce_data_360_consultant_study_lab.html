How to reason

Trace the full data lifecycle: business purpose, source and latency, DLO, transformation, DMO mapping, identity, insight, segment, activation, governance, and consumption. Choose the option that fixes the earliest root cause and remains secure and maintainable.

Exam habits

Several answers may sound technically possible. Watch for grain, stable keys, relationship paths, consent freshness, false matches, destination identifiers, and end-to-end latency. “Real time” and “more data” are not automatically the best design.

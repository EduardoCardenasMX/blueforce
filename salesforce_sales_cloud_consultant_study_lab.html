How to reason

Identify the business outcome, constraints, operating model, data implications, and long-term maintainability. Then choose the solution that best balances standard capability, risk, adoption, and measurable value.

Exam habits

Several options may be technically possible. Read qualifiers such as “best,” “most scalable,” “first,” and “most appropriate,” then select the choice that addresses the root cause and the full scenario.
